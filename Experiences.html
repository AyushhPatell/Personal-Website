Internship Experience at CRA

Role: IT Support Co-op - Fall 2024

During the Fall 2024 semester, I completed an exciting internship at the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) in Summerside, PEI. As an IT Support Co-op, I was responsible for several tasks, including:

  • Installing and configuring hardware and software components.
  • Troubleshooting network issues and system malfunctions.
  • Maintaining the inventory of hardware and software resources.
  • Providing technical assistance and advice to employees.

Key Projects:

  1. Network Upgrade Project: Assisted in upgrading CRA’s network infrastructure, improving speed and security for over 500 users.
  2. Asset Management System: Created an automated system to track and update hardware inventory, significantly reducing manual errors.
  3. Technical Training: Conducted training sessions for employees on new software installations and best IT practices.

Skills Gained:

  • Network Administration
  • Hardware and Software Troubleshooting
  • Technical Documentation
  • Customer Support and Communication

My experience at CRA allowed me to apply my classroom knowledge in a real-world setting and strengthened my skills in IT support, problem-solving, and teamwork. This internship has been a significant step toward my career goal of becoming a proficient IT professional.
